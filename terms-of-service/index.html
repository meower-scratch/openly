Terms

Section 1: Introduction

Welcome to Openly! We hope you enjoy our service. There are some rules you must follow, and breaking these rules may result in moderation action being taken against your account.

Section 2: Warning Points

Once you violate the Terms of Service (hereinafter "ToS"), Warning Points will be added to your account.

Warning Points are given on a scale of 1-10 depending on the severity of the rule.

  1. Extremely Minor Violation
  2. Very Minor Violation
  3. Minor Violation
  4. Slightly Moderate Violation
  5. Moderate Violation
  6. Intermediate Violation
  7. High Violation
  8. Higher Violation
  9. Final Warning
  10. Unacceptable Violation

Breaking a rule that earns more than 3 Warning Points will result in a personal warning from a moderator. Getting 10 Warning Points in total on your account will result in you getting banned for a certain period of time, and getting more than 10 Warning Points will result in a permanent ban (hereinafter "termination") on your account.

Being banned does not reset your Warning Points, so another broken rule would result in a termination.
